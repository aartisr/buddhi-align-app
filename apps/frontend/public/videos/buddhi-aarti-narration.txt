Hi, I am Aarti, and I will quickly show you how to use Buddhi Align.
Start on the Home dashboard. I check Daily Rings first, then jump into the module I want.
In Karma Yoga, I log one action and the impact every day.
In Bhakti Journal, I write one reflection and one gratitude point.
In Motivation and Analytics, I review streak, total entries, and trend charts.
In Settings, I manage language, music, and export or import my data.
As owner, in Admin I review incidents, experiments, audit logs, and system errors.
My daily routine is simple: dashboard, two entries, analytics review, one improvement step.
Thanks for watching this quick tour. Let us grow with consistency.
